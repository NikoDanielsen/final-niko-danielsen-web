This is a travel website about my time and adventures in Greece 2023. Below is a map of where I sailed around Greece whilst upon a 'SunSail' company sailboat. This expedetion was a 5 Night 6 Day experience and we stopped at little islands along the way. Such as Paros, Lagos. etc. Greece is located in South Eastern Europe, bordering the Lonian Sea and the Mediterranean Sea. It is a peninsular country, with an archipelago of about 3000 Islands.
