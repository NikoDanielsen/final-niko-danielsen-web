Greek Cuisine

Food

Greece has a very unique cuisine that they take pride in as it is said in Greece, "Food is part of the Greek lifestyle"

In Greece at your local corner store/takeaway store they will serve traditional Greek meals, these are Gyros, Briam, Keftedes, and Tzatiki. Tzaiki although being just a sauce is used on almost every meat dish in Greece. consistantly throughout traditional Greek meals they will always use a 'meat' in them as Greek people love meat. This can be either fish lamb chicken octopus etc.
